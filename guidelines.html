Anyone applying for drone permissions can get their drone permit from chief district office of the proposed project location, if they are flying a drone less than 2 km.

Government of Nepal has different concern for drone operation.

Civil Aviation Authority of Nepal (CAAN )

CAAN is mainly concerned about collision with other aircrafts i.e. safety. They believe that drone flying poses security threats, weakens air safety and encroaches personal privacy.

Ministry of Information and Communication

They are concerned about the frequency of the remote control of the device used. This frequency might create interference with devices being used by security personals and communication.

Home Ministry

Drones in its original terms means the vehicles which are being used by military to strike weapons in different areas. As such, the home ministry which looks after country’s security is worried if people use drones for illegal activities.

Relevant Line Ministries

Drones in its original terms means the vehicles which are being used by military to strike weapons in different areas. As such, the home ministry which looks after country’s security is worried if people use drones for illegal activities.

"Beside above agencies, line agencies needs also to be contacted for drone permissions eg. National parks department for flying in protected areas, ministry of tourism for movie shoot in mountains.

As per the recent regulations of Nepal, the permissions are granted to the local government. So any agencies can give the permissions according to the flight plan and spot."

Special conditions for operating Drone and restricted fly zone in Nepal

  • check_circle No need to get CAAN for permission if the drone is under 2 kg (4.4 lb) and you are using it under 200m (656 feet) on your personal properties like inside your house, land, and garden.
  • check_circle Drone must be flown within the visual line of sight (normally within 500 m horizontally and 400 feet vertically) of its remote pilot (the person in charge of operating drone). Operations beyond these distances must be approved by the CAAN (the basic premise being for the operator to prove that he/she can fly it safely).
  • check_circle Unmanned aircraft shall be permitted to be operated in day VMC conditions only.
  • check_circle CAAN permission is required for all flights that are being conducted for aerial work subject to the approval of concerned Authority having jurisdiction over such aerial work.
  • check_circle If the operations are to be conducted in a Restricted or Prohibited area, prior approval from the concerned Authority is required before applying for CAAN permission.
  • check_circle Airport areas including take-off and approach paths shall be free of all UAV activity at all times regardless of altitude.
  • check_circle To use drone in Nepal, you cannot get permission for longer period of time like for a year but can get for short period of time.
